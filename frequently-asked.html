Frequently Asked Questions



How do I use uPrintHub machine?

Registered Client

  1. Upload your file to this website and remember the document code or bring your flash drive when using the printing machine.

  2. Get file by choosing the file on your flashdrive or get your file online via document code.

  3. Check the document and set print settings.

  4. The machine will ask for your username and pin to check your remaining credits from previous transactions.

  5. Insert the asked amount of coins by the machine. We save your changes as credits that you can use for later transactions.

  6. Press PRINT button and wait for your document to be printed.

Can I use uPrintHub machine without an account?

Yes you can! Here's how.

  1. Plug your removable storage and select the desired document to be printed from your flashdrive.

  2. Check the document and set print settings.

  3. Insert the asked amount of coins by the machine. Our machines do not give changes or keep credits to unregistered clients.
    Register now its free!

  4. Press PRINT button and your document will be printed in a few moments.

How to use uPrintHub website?

  1. Login with your account on our website.

  2. Register if you still haven't registered.

  3. Drag and drop your files to upload documents. You can also click the UPLOAD button and select your files.Maximum allowed size of files(batch upload) is no greater than 20MB.

  4. Remember or write-down the reference code for your document. We'll also email you the code just in case you lost it.

What are the supported documents?

  1. Our printing machine and website accepts only PDF documents as of the time.

  2. PDF Documents preserves the appearance of your document and avoid unexpected changes in layout and other rendering issues.

  3. Heads up! We will also support word document formats such as .doc, .docx, .rtf & more on our commercial launch soon!
